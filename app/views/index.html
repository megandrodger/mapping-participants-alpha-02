This is a reference tool for you to support finding out and recording the digital inclusion category of your research participants.

Below are suggestions of questions and activities that could be used during your research session to help expose signifiers of digital confidence. Please refer to the digital inclusion information for reminders of each category.

Activities and questions


Activity

Ask your participant to download the consent form for the session from an email. Ask them to fill it out online and attach it to an email to send back


Question

“Please would you talk me through how you/what you use your phone day to day?”


Activity

Ask your participant to copy the information they are being tested with from an online/digital source


Question

“How do you use your phone/computer mostly; is it mainly for work, social arrangement, or entertainment?”


Activity

Ask your participant to change the settings on their browser


Activity

Ask your participant to save a bookmark, and then later come back to it
